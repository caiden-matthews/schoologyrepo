Built for Students, by Students

Study Planner was created to solve a real problem: students using Schoology often feel overwhelmed by fragmented assignment information spread across the platform. Classes, due dates, and grades are hard to see at a glance.

We built Study Planner to give you one organized view of everything that matters—your assignments, deadlines, and grades. No clutter. No distractions. Just what you need to succeed.

Whether you're managing multiple classes, preparing for exams, or just trying to stay organized, Study Planner helps you take control of your academic life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. We use industry-standard security (HTTPS encryption) to protect your data in transit. Your Schoology credentials are never stored by us—we only store the access token needed to sync your assignments and grades. You can disconnect at any time, and all data is immediately removed from our servers.
Do you share my data with anyone? ▼
No. Your data is yours. We never share, sell, or give your assignment or grade information to anyone—including advertisers or third-party services. We only use your data to show you your own assignments and grades within the app.
Why Schoology? ▼
Schoology is the platform used by millions of K–12 students, but it can be hard to navigate and organize your work. Study Planner gives you a cleaner, simpler interface designed specifically for students who want to stay organized and on top of their assignments.
How does syncing work? ▼
When you first connect your Schoology account, Study Planner syncs all your assignments, grades, and calendar events. After that, you can manually sync anytime using the "Sync" button, or data updates automatically as you use the app. Syncing is fast and only takes a few seconds.
Is Study Planner free? ▼
Yes! Study Planner is completely free to use. No hidden costs, no premium upgrades needed. We support the app through non-intrusive ads that help us keep the service running.
